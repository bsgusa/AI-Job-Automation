All data processed by Lexora AI is:
- Encrypted with AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit
- **Never** used to train our models
- Isolated per tenant with zero data co-mingling
- Available in your jurisdiction (US, EU, UK, APAC)

Certifications: **SOC 2 Type II · ISO 27001 · GDPR · HIPAA · CCPA · FedRAMP**
