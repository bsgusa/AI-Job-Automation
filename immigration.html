Priority dates apply to family-based and employment-based immigration where annual visa quotas exist. Your priority date is the date USCIS received your initial petition. If your category is oversubscribed (common for certain countries), you may need to wait until your priority date becomes "current" before your green card can be approved.
An RFE is a USCIS request for additional information to decide your case. They must be answered within the deadline (typically 87 days) or your case may be denied. Lexora AI specializes in crafting thorough, well-documented RFE responses that address all USCIS concerns and maximize your approval chances.
